About

We are focusing on awareness - rather the lack thereof - surrounding topics that deal with sex, depression, and other topics of that nature in minority neighborhoods. Our goal is to create an interactive website that connects teenagers/adults to resources that provides them with help to these problems; educate the public to recognize people with these physiological issues and interact with them positively.

Not knowing is a dilemma in our society, this website works to end the unknown as it relates to these issues, we are creating a platform in which information is shared between patients, professionals and anyone with a positive outlook on these physiological problems.

Depression

Depression is one of the most common mental illness it affects more than 16 million of Americans each year and more than 350 million people in the world. Here are some ways to deal with Depression and some hotlines to talk to experts.

Tips & Suggestions:

  • Tip 1: Reach out and stay connected

  • Tip 2: Do things that make you feel good

  • Tip 3: Move vigorously during the day

  • Tip 4: Eat a heathly, mood boosting diet

  • Tip 6: Get a Daily dose of sunlight

For more tips on dealing with depressions visit this website:
